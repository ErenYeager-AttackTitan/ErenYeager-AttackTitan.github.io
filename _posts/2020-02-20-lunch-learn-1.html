What is Design Thinking?

Design Thinking is a creative problem solving technique with the user at the centre. As Steve Jobs famously said:

You have to start with the customer experience and work backwards to the technology - not the other way around.

Design Thinking challenges us to start with understanding and empathizing with the end user’s pain points. By understanding the users needs and wants, Apple was able to re-imagine and create products that set itself apart from its competitors. During our stay at MC, we will also be using this methodology to help us create an impactful digital solution for our end users.
