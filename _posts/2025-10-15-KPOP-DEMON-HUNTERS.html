K-Pop Demon Hunters: K-Pop Meets Demon-Slaying

Published on 15 October 2025, 15:30 IST

Introduction

K-Pop Demon Hunters is a 2025 animated musical fantasy film produced by Sony Pictures Animation and released on Netflix. The story follows the K-pop girl group HUNTR/X—Rumi, Mira, and Zoey—as they lead double lives as demon hunters, protecting humanity from supernatural threats while maintaining their pop star personas.

Plot Overview

The trio's world turns upside down when they discover that their rival boy band, the Saja Boys, are actually demons bent on unleashing chaos. Blending high-energy K-pop performances with intense action sequences, the film explores themes of identity, friendship, and the battle between good and evil.

Characters & Key Scenes

The movie focuses on the dynamic between the three heroines, their rival band, and the demons they fight. Fans are treated to visually stunning sequences of both music performances and supernatural battles.
