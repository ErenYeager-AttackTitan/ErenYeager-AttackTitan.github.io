Top Trending Anime of Fall 2024: Devil May Cry Takes the Lead

Published on 2024-10-23

Introduction

The Fall 2024 anime season is stacked with exciting releases, and *Devil May Cry* is leading the charge! Here’s a look at the anime making waves this season.

1. Devil May Cry

The iconic demon hunter Dante returns in a brand-new *Devil May Cry* anime by Netflix and Studio Mir. Fans are excited to see Dante’s stylish demon-slaying action in a fresh new storyline.

2. Blue Lock (Season 2)

Returning for its second season, *Blue Lock* continues to captivate with intense soccer action as players fight to become the world’s greatest striker.

3. Blue Exorcist: Beyond the Snow Saga

*Blue Exorcist* brings more supernatural drama this fall, with Rin and his friends facing new challenges that make it perfect for the spooky season.

4. Dragon Ball DAIMA

In this new *Dragon Ball* adventure, Goku and Vegeta are transformed into kids again, launching them into a mysterious world of adventure and danger.

5. Tomb Raider: The Legend of Lara Croft

*Tomb Raider* fans can look forward to Lara Croft’s anime series, blending the action-adventure excitement of the games with anime’s visual style.

6. DAN DA DAN

This supernatural horror-romance anime has captured fans’ attention with its mix of eerie visuals and a gripping storyline.

7. Days with My Stepsister

This heartfelt slice-of-life anime delves into family dynamics and relationships, with emotional depth that resonates with viewers.
