About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the School of Mathematics, Cardiff University working under Prof. Roger Behrend.
I completed my doctoral studies at the Faculty of Mathematics, University of Vienna under the supervision of Prof. Ilse Fischer in October, 2019. Prior to that, I did a Postgraduate Diploma from The Abdus Salam ICTP, Trieste (Italy) in 2015. During 2009 - 2014, I did my masters in mathematics from Tezpur University, India.
