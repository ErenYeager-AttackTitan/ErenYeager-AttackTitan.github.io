About Me

I am a Research Fellow in the Combinatorics Group of the Fakultät für Mathematik, Universität Wien working under Prof. Ilse Fischer. I completed my Postgraduate Diploma from The Abdus Salam ICTP, Trieste (Italy) in 2015. Prior to this (2009 - 2014), I did my masters in mathematics from Tezpur University, India.
