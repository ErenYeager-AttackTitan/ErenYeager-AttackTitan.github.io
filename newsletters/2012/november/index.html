  • Girish Krishnan Ramdas, a student at NID, Gandhinagar is interning with us for a month. He will be working on a safety awareness game.

Proposals and Projects

 U-  Urban Systems P-  Public Safety and Security G-  Games S-  Simulations V-  Visualisations

  • Smart Campus- U, S
    The first phase of the project concluded on November 17th, 2012. In this phase, we conducted surveys and interviews to understand user behaviour & preferences, and began development of a web application to aid in energy accounting. A report of activities for this phase has been submitted to IIIT-B.
  • Awareness Game about Cycle Lanes- P, G
    We have begun working on an awareness game about the recently introduced cycle lanes in Jayanagar. Currently we are conducting surveys to understand people's opinions about these cycle lanes.
  • Enhancing Solidarity: Unpacking women's work in public spaces using games, simulation, and visualisation - U, S, G,V
    We submitted the above titled proposal to the ESRC-DFID "Development Frontiers Research Fund, 2012-13" Call for Proposals. The overall objective of this proposal is to examine the dynamics and experiences of women's lives who work in urban public spaces, and to use the concepts of games, visualisations and simulations to create a platform for women to interact and to generate & disseminate knowledge.
  • Cities of Hope: Examining the evolution of slums through health systems perspective - U,S
    We submitted the above titled proposal to DFID-ESRC joint fund for poverty alleviation research, phase 3. The aim of the proposal was to examine the different ways in which the evolution and growth of slums in the city of Bangalore, India, have a critical impact on the manner and extent to which policies for reducing poverty are implemented, especially in the area of Health.
