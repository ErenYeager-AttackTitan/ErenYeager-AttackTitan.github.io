About Me

Hello, I'm Ashutosh!

Currenlty working as a Senior Data Scientist at HDFC Life with Data Labs (Predictive Modelling team). Majority of my work includes generating actionable business insights through applied machine learning, which involves collecting data from various sources, cleaning, pre-processing, outlier detection, feature engineering, ML algorithm selection, hyper parameter tuning, algorithm training, validation and model deployment for the end users.

Previously, I had worked with Flytxt as a Data Scientist, where I developed machine learning solution for clients (Telecom Companies) with the application of Applied machine learning and Big data fundamentals (Apache-Spark, HDFS, Distributed Computing).

Regarding my education background, I did Masters in Technology in Industrial Engineering and Operations Research (IEOR), IIT Bombay, where my research work was on Classifying Retinal Images using Deep learning (Computer Vision), which was also published in a Image and signal processsing conference. My areas of interest lies in Classical and Bayesian machine learning techniques, Causal Inference, Explainable AI and Deep learning.
