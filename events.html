In 2015, 193 member states of the United Nations adopted a development agenda for 2030, which included a list of 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). (http://www.globalgoals.org/)

This year, the first ever Global Goals Jam is being held in 15 cities across the world, where participants will design interventions with these SDGs as the focus. In India, the Global Goals Jam will be held at Fields of View, Bangalore on 17th and 18th September. We invite practitioners from diverse disciplines to join us in designing the path to a sustainable future.

In the two days, participants will work in teams and design together. The goals we have chosen are -
1. SDG 1 - No Poverty
2. SDG 5 - Gender Equality
3. SDG 11 - Sustainable Cities and Communities

We also promise to keep you going with some delicious uttara karnataka cuisine, nippattus and chai!
